Site Purpose

The primary purpose of weathergo.net is to provide users with accurate and up to date weather forecasts for select cities.

This will be accomplished by allowing users to visit the site and select their city from a dropdown selection of available cities. They can then not only view the current weather for that location, but the predicted weather forecast as well.

The goal of this site is to inform its users of what they can expect with regard to current and upcoming weather so that they can be better prepared now and in the coming days.
